Saturday January 21, 2006

Things I didn’t do today: Give that section of the house a second coat of paint. Things I did do today: Bring the garbage to the dump. Clean the bathroom. I guess I did some of the ‘dirtier’ things. It was drizzling out, so it was a bad day to paint. It was also a bad day to go to the dump, but that didn’t matter. I watched Lawrence of Arabia tonight. It was a good movie. Very interesting. And the hero is not perfect at all, he has his flaws and his incredible points. He basically becomes a legend by doing a whole series of impossible feats as a leader, but he leans in and out of insanity more and more as the film progresses. It’s really neat to see a ‘honest’ portrayal of a flawed hero. Ryan Originally posted at classicalRyan.xanga.com
